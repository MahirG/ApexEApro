Full Strategy Disclosure

Know exactly what you're running before you run it

Most forex robots hide their logic behind buzzwords. Apex EA Pro documents it, because an EA you don't understand is an EA you'll switch off at the worst moment.

01 · Entry Logic

Session-based mean reversion

The EA trades the London and New York sessions only. It waits for price to sweep liquidity beyond a prior session extreme on EURUSD, GBPUSD, USDJPY or XAUUSD, confirms rejection on the M15 close, then enters with a resting limit order — never chasing market price.

02 · Risk Per Trade

Fixed fractional, hard stops

Every position risks a fixed fraction of current equity — 0.5% by default, capped at 1%. The stop loss is placed with the entry order at the broker, not managed "virtually" in the terminal. If your VPS dies mid-trade, your stop still exists.

03 · Exit & Filters

Structured exits, news-aware

Targets are set at 1.5–2.5R based on measured volatility (ATR). A built-in economic calendar filter flattens or blocks entries around high-impact releases like NFP, CPI and rate decisions. Prop Mode closes everything before Friday's session end.

What Apex EA Pro will never do

These are the techniques that make an equity curve look perfect until the account is gone. They're excluded at the code level — not just "turned off."

Martingale lot-sizing after losses
Grid / averaging into drawdown
Trading without a stop loss
Hidden or "virtual-only" stops
Latency / broker arbitrage tricks
Promises of guaranteed returns
Verified Results & Testing Methodology

How we test — and how you can check us

A backtest is only as honest as its assumptions. Ours are documented below, and the live account exists so you never have to take the backtest's word for it.

99.9% modelling-quality tick data. Backtests use Dukascopy tick data with variable real spreads — not MT4's default 90% interpolated bars that make weak strategies look flawless.

Slippage and commission modelled. Every simulated fill includes 0.3 pips average slippage and $7/lot round-turn commission — the same costs you'll pay on a real ECN account.

Out-of-sample validation. Parameters were optimized on 2015–2019 data only, then locked. Everything from 2020 onward — including the entire live period — is out-of-sample.

Live-to-backtest tracking error published. The live account has tracked the backtest within ±0.4% per month. If those ever diverge materially, we say so in the changelog — publicly.
